# 🐉 Dragon's Den — Pitch Script

## Golden Ivory | "HealthUp" Presentation

---

### 🎤 ACT I — INTRO

**Ghaja** walks confidently to the center of the chamber, gives a respectful nod to the Dragons

**Ghaja**: "Greetings, Dragons. My name is Ghaja, founder and head of Golden Ivory. Thank you for having me here today."

---

### ⚠️ ACT II — THE PROBLEM

**Ghaja**: "Let me ask you something. You're mid-battle. A troll's axe has found your ribs. Your allies are occupied. You reach into your pack — and what do you find?

Six. Glass. Vials.

You fumble through them. One slips. It shatters on the dungeon floor. That's gold — and hit points — gone in an instant.

Health potions are a cornerstone of survival in this world. Nobody disputes that. But the form they come in? Fragile glass containers that clink, crack, and clutter your inventory? Managing a dozen individual vials mid-adventure is not a system — it's a hazard.

Every adventurer, soldier, field medic, and caravan guard knows this pain. The potion works. The packaging doesn't."

---

### 💡 ACT III — THE PITCH

**Ghaja**: "That is exactly the problem Golden Ivory set out to solve.

Ladies and gentlemen of the Den — allow me to introduce: HealthUp.

*(produces a Health Pack from their satchel and holds it up)*

This is a Health Pack. Compact. Durable. Fits on your belt, in your bag, or strapped to your forearm. Each pack holds up to six H-Ups — that's what we call our individual HealthUp units.

Now — what is an H-Up? Think of it as a gusher. A soft, bite-sized chewable infused with restorative essence. No glass. No cork. No spillage. You pop one in your mouth, and within moments, you've recovered the equivalent of a Healing Word — right there on the battlefield, no action wasted fumbling with a stopper.

Six H-Ups per pack. One pack on your person. That's six emergency recoveries in a form factor smaller than a coin purse.

But here's what truly sets us apart from every potion vendor in the market:

The Health Pack is reusable.

You don't throw it away when it's empty. You refill it. We sell H-Ups in bulk — affordable, portable refill pouches you can restock at any town, apothecary, or directly through Golden Ivory's distribution partners. Wherever you rest, wherever you resupply — your Health Pack gets topped up and you're ready to go again.

We are not selling a consumable. We are selling a system."

---

### 🏁 ACT IV — THE FINISHER

**Ghaja**: "Golden Ivory was built on one belief: that survival tools should work as hard as the people who carry them.

H-Ups are faster, safer, lighter, and smarter than anything currently on the market. And with the renewable pack model, we create a loyal customer base that comes back — not because they have to, but because nothing else compares.

*(pauses, looks each Dragon in the eye)*

Dragons — in a world where your next battle is always one wrong turn away…

'Don't drink to survive. Just HealthUp.'

I'm seeking investment to scale production and expand distribution across the major trade routes. I look forward to your questions.

Thank you."

*[Ghaja gives a composed bow and steps back, Health Pack still in hand]*
